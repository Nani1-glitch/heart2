Men are at higher risk for heart disease at an earlier age than women. The risk of a heart attack begins to increase more rapidly when a man reaches age 45. A woman's risk of heart attack begins to increase when she reaches age 55. The hormone estrogen is protective to the heart, so when a woman reaches menopause and her estrogen production reduces, discuss HRT with your physician.
