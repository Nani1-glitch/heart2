Thalassemia is a genetic disease. This means that a person can only get Thalassemia disease or trait by inheriting the genes for Thalassemia from their parents. Genes determine what we look like, such as hair color, and are also responsible for many diseases. Thalassemia trait is not and never will become Thalassemia disease. There are two main types of Thalassemia trait: Alpha Thalassemia trait and Beta Thalassemia trait
